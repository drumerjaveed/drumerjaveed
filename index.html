About

Researcher.
Practitioner.
Educator.

With a PhD in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I work at the intersection of research rigour and practical execution. My focus is helping leadership teams understand, implement, and govern AI systems — responsibly and effectively.

I've guided organisations through the full AI adoption lifecycle — from readiness assessments and strategy design, through tool selection, workflow integration, and team enablement. I also conduct independent AI audits to ensure your systems are explainable, fair, and aligned with your risk appetite.

Whether you're a C-suite leader cautiously eyeing AI adoption, or a team already in the weeds of implementation, I bring clarity, structure, and hands-on expertise to every engagement.

What I Do

Services

Three tightly focused offerings designed to meet organisations at their exact stage of the AI journey.

01

AI Implementation Lead

I design and oversee end-to-end AI integration into your workflows — from architecture and tool selection to change management and go-live. You get a senior technical partner who speaks both the language of business outcomes and engineering constraints.

02

AI Audit & Governance

Independent assessment of your existing or planned AI systems for bias, explainability, data quality, security posture, and regulatory alignment. I deliver actionable audit reports your board can trust and your teams can act on.

03

AI Consulting & Training

Bespoke workshops and advisory programmes that upskill your teams — from AI literacy sessions for executives to hands-on implementation training for practitioners. I tailor depth and focus to your industry, role, and readiness level.

How I Work

Engagement Process

A structured approach that removes ambiguity and maximises value from day one.

01

Discovery Call

A focused conversation to understand your organisation's context, goals, pain points, and current AI maturity. No obligation — just clarity on whether there's a fit and what shape the engagement might take.

02

Readiness Assessment

A structured review of your data infrastructure, team capabilities, existing tools, and strategic priorities. This forms the foundation of a realistic and responsible AI roadmap.

03

Strategy & Design

Co-creating an implementation plan or audit scope with your leadership team — with clear milestones, success metrics, and risk mitigations built in from the start.

04

Execution & Enablement

Hands-on delivery: leading implementation, conducting the audit, or running workshops — with regular progress checkpoints and documentation throughout.

05

Review & Handover

A thorough debrief, final deliverables, and a clear path for your team to maintain and build on the work independently. I measure success by your self-sufficiency, not dependency on me.
